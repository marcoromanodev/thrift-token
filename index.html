Transforming Fashion Waste

The global fashion industry produces over 92 million tons of textile waste annually, a number projected to grow by 60% by 2030. Thrift Token integrates advanced fiber separation technologies, blockchain transparency, and a gamified experience to build a circular textile economy that reduces waste and addresses clothing inequality.

Fiber Separation Technology

  • Chemical recycling methods to safely separate synthetic and natural fibers.
  • Mechanical processes with advanced sorting for blended fabrics.
  • AI-powered assessment to identify optimal recycling paths for different textiles.

Thrift Network Hubs

  • Garment drop-off points where users receive Thrift Tokens for donations.
  • Recycling centers using cutting-edge fiber separation technology.
  • Redistribution hubs that clean and deliver garments to underserved populations.
  • Community engagement through educational workshops and sustainability events.
